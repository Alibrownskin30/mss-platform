Methodology

MSS Protocol focuses on structural risk — signals that often exist before problems become obvious.

Authorities

  • Mint Authority: if present, supply can be changed (risk).
  • Freeze Authority: if present, transfers may be restricted (risk).

Concentration

  • Top1 / Top5 / Top10 / Top20 are calculated from largest token accounts.
  • Owner is resolved from token account data where possible.

Market Context

  • Price / liquidity / volume / FDV come from public market sources.
  • MCap is computed as price × totalSupplyUi when direct market cap is unavailable.

Clusters

Beta clusters are a best-effort snapshot. “Clusters” here means repeated ownership across top token accounts, which can indicate structuring. This is not a definitive conclusion.

This methodology will expand as historical tracking, alerting, and deeper on-chain heuristics are enabled.
